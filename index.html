Private by design: recording, editing, and storage all happen in your browser — audio never leaves your machine. Desktop capture needs Chrome or Edge and the “Share audio” checkbox in the picker (tab share = tab audio; screen share = system audio on Windows). Clips persist in this browser; use Save project for a portable backup. Ctrl+Z undoes pattern edits, Commit trim, and clip deletes.
